Welcome to Globus!

Globus is a high-performance data-transfer paltform. You can use Globus for…

Data transfers between servers in your group.
Data transfers between a server and your laptop.
Sharing data with researchers at other institutions.
Sharing data with the world.
Automate data transfers between any of the above.

Transfers happen unattended, data verification is on by default, and on-the-wire encryption is available.

All of this is available now, for you, for free.

 

Get Started

If you have a data-sharing problem, or a collaboration problem, our "How Do I…?" page will have a solution!

If you know that you need the Globus client, go directly to our client page.

If you know that you need the Globus server, you should still probably check out the "How Do I…?" page , but if you already know exactly what you need, you can head directly to our server page.

What Is Globus?

Globus is a platform for high-speed data transfer. If you have Low Risk data, you can use Globus to share it with people in Stanford, with collaborators at other institutions, or with the general public.
